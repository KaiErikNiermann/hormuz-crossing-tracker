## AIS (Automatic Identification System) Data

Live vessel positions derived from public AIS radio broadcasts via [AISStream.io](https://aisstream.io/).

- **License**: AIS is a public broadcast system mandated by the IMO. Aggregated and processed AIS data is not subject to copyright restrictions.
